# Safety and Scope

## OSINT-Only Boundaries

This application is for public-data situational awareness only.

- No targeting of individuals.
- No doxxing workflows.
- No weapons guidance.
- No operational violence planning.
- No covert or unauthorized intrusion guidance.

## Permitted Use

- Monitoring public events, policy shifts, disasters, and market signals.
- Aggregating public RSS/API feeds and displaying trends.
- Building deterministic alerts for organizational awareness.

## Guardrails Implemented

- Connector model is restricted to public/free APIs/RSS by default.
- Optional key-based connectors are feature-flagged and disabled without keys.
- System is designed for resilience and observability, not offensive capability.
